Introduction

Inglayan Peanut butter originated from Bila, Bauko Mountain Province which is a spread made from either ground ow whole dry roasted peanuts. It is commonly used in sandwiches, cookies, pastries. Inglayan Peanut butter comes in creamy and crunchyy varieties, that has its own unique texture and taste.

It is a popular spread in many cultures due to its rich flavor, versatility, and nutritional benefits.

In this section, we will explore the various aspects of Inglayan peanut butter, including its health benefits, usage in recipes, and more.

Ingredients: Peanuts from Bauko, Salt, Sugar, Hydrogenated Vegetable Oils
Health Benefits

Inglayan Peanut butter is rich in protein, healthy fats, and various vitamins and minerals. Some of its health benefits include:

  • High in protein, which helps with muscle growth and repair.
  • Contains healthy fats, which are good for heart health.
  • Rich in vitamins like Vitamin E, which is good for the skin.
  • Good source of antioxidants that can help fight inflammation.
  • High in magnesium, which supports nerve and muscle function.

While Inglayan peanut butter is nutritious, it's important to consume it in moderation due to its high calorie content.

Recommended serving size: 1-2 tablespoons (32g) depending on your taste.
Recipes

Inglayan Peanut butter is incredibly versatile in the kitchen. Here are a few simple recipes you can try:

  • Peanut Butter Sandwich: Spread Inglayan peanut butter on whole-grain bread. Match it with liquids(milk, water, carbonated drinks) for enjoyment.
  • Peanut Butter Smoothie: Blend the product with bananas, milk, and ice for a creamy smoothie.
  • Peanut Butter Cookies: Mix Inglayan peanut butter, sugar, and egg to make simple, delicious cookies.
  • Peanut Butter and Banana Toast: Spread Inglayan peanut butter on toast and top with banana slices.
  • Peanut Butter Granola Bars: Combine Inglayan peanut butter with oats, honey, and chocolate chips to make homemade granola bars.

Storage

Proper storage of peanut butter ensures it stays fresh and maintains its quality. Here are some tips:

  • Store creamy Inglayan peanut butter in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight.
  • For crunchy Inglayan peanut butter, store it in the refrigerator to prevent separation of the oil.
  • When trying to create a replica of the product, It should be refrigerated to extend shelf life.
