Teacher Jennifer Lapinas

The Founder of the Inglayan Peanut Butter located at Bila, Bauko Mountain Province

Ma'am Jennifer Lapinas is a current High School Teacher from Bila, Bauko Mountain Province. Aside from nurturing and molding minds of the future generation, the entrepreneural mindset of Ma'am Jennifer has created an impact not only to the folks of Bauko but also to the livelihood of the Province.

Pandemic striked it was when Inglayan Peanut Butter started. Later on, recognized by the Department of Science and Technology and Department of Tourism then continues to expand up to the present. Due to bunch of orders from different locals, It had opened economic strategies like releasing new flavors to the market which are creamy and crunchy flavors, and Introduced the folks opportunity to be part of the businesses's industry. From "Kiniing" to "Kalkalot" Bauko Mountain Province is now also known to its creamy and crunchy peanut butter delicacy.
